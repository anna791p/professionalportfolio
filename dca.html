Dan Church Aid Campaign

Client: Dan Church Aid

Collaboration: Emily, Alma, Lana, Paulina

Tools: Premiere Pro, Audition

Role: Conceptualization, filming, audio

Dan Church Aid is an organization that provides support, aid and relief to countries and people who suffer from disaster and poverty.

They have a campaign known as 'Det er dansk at dele' or 'It is Danish to share' and they gave us the task of creating a video campaign that would encourage more sharing in Denmark.

We created a full length campaign and then cut it down into a bumper that can be used for social media.
