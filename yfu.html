YFU Website and Campaign

Client: YFU (Youth For Understanding)

Collaboration: Celia, Elisabet, Ekaterina, Yan, Emily

Tools: Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, HTML, CSS, Javascript

Role: Video Editing, Conceptualization

YFU (Youth For Understanding) isa non-profit organization that offer exchange experiences mainly to 14-18 year olds. They were not able to reach an older target audience 18+ and thus they came to us, for us to come up with a solution to make a campaign that would target and reach the older audience.

In this process we had to design a website, campaign video and create a digital media solution which they could use to target the older audiences. Upon thorough research of interviews, surveys and more research, we decided upon instagram, snapchat and facebook.
