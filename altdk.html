ALT.dk Explainer

Client: Egmont Publishing

Collaboration: Wilma, Nynne

Tools: Adobe After Effects

Role: Conceptualization, Motion Groups

Egmont Publishing is one of the largest media groups in Scandinavia and they are responsible for publishing over 700 magazines titles. They publish prominent Danish magazines such as ‘ALT for Damerne’ and ‘Eurowoman’. They own alt.dk - one of the most popular lifestyle websites in Scandinavia - however, they had some branding issues with it and many people were associating alt.dk as a part of some of the popular magazines. Our task was to create a motion graphics explainer that would strengthen and expose the visual identity of alt.dk, as well as explaining to users what alt.dk do, in a fun and creative way.
